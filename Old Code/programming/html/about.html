About Us

Atom combines the visual aesthetic capabilities of a design studio with the brand, marketing and communications knowledge of a traditional agency. This is enhanced with our in-house digital expertise and a solid understanding of how to operate in today’s online arena.

The philosophy of the agency is based around creating great work that achieves our clients’ objectives. Please view our work via our service categories of Brand, Design, Digital and Media, or select on a client from the menu to view the individual project.

What We Do
